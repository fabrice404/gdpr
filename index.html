GDPR email generator
Source code

Fill in the form on the left, and click generate button. It will generate an email to ask your personal data information.

Your personal data is only used to generate this email template, it's not sent anywhere and stays on your computer. Your can clear data stored on your computer by clicking the button "Clear data" on the top right corner.

Your usage of this tool is not tracked, and the source code is available on GitHub.
